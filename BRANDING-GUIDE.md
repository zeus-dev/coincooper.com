# Branding Guide — CoinCooper & Aetherion Exchange

## Brand Hierarchy

```
CoinCooper (Parent Company)
    ↓
Aetherion Exchange (Product/Software)
```

## Logo Usage

### Navigation Bar
- **Logo**: CoinCooper logo (Coincooper-transparant.png)
- **Text**: 
  - "CoinCooper" (company name)
  - "Aetherion Exchange" (product name, smaller)
- **Purpose**: Establishes CoinCooper as the parent company, Aetherion as the product

### Hero Section
- **Badge**: "Powered by CoinCooper"
- **Purpose**: Immediately communicates the relationship

### Footer
- **Copyright**: "© 2026 CoinCooper. All rights reserved."
- **Tagline**: "Aetherion Exchange — Built with 🔥 by CoinCooper"
- **Purpose**: Reinforces CoinCooper ownership and Aetherion as the product

## Messaging Strategy

### Homepage (coincooper.com)
This is a **marketing site** showcasing:
- CoinCooper as the company
- Aetherion Exchange as the flagship product
- RPC Node services as secondary offering

### Deployment Strategy

**coincooper.com** (This Website)
- Marketing & sales site
- Showcases Aetherion Exchange product
- Explains features, architecture, pricing
- Generates leads for demos

**aetherion.exchange** (Separate Deployment)
- Live demo of the exchange
- Actual trading interface
- Shows what customers will get
- "Powered by CoinCooper" branding
